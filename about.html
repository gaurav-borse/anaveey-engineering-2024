Anaveey Engineering Pvt. Ltd.

About Us

Founded in 2018, Anaveey Engineering Pvt. Ltd. is a technology-driven organization engaged in Engineering, Design and Development of Steel Products. We manufacture various types of Storage Tanks, Chemical Plant Equipment, Acoustic Enclosures and Containers, and Heavy Fabrication Products.

We are a company that works on the virtues of innovation and customization while keeping in harmony with the global environment. We provide a high-quality range of services with full customization as per client's specifications, catering to a variety of sectors worldwide. Utilizing premium quality raw materials and cutting-edge equipment, we have gained the loyalty and trust of our clients based in domestic and overseas markets.

Based on strategic planning, industry best practices, client input, and reliable business procedures, we produce high-quality products on time, satisfy client needs, and work towards continuous improvement.

We are strong believers in punctuality and meet the organizational tasks within the scheduled time in an effective manner. We have gained a respectable place in this fiercely competitive business under the visionary guidance of our director. We are fulfilling customer requirements with our diligent labour and hard work.

We address customer demands and expectations by following a results-oriented approach. We regularly interact with our customers to ensure quality, delivery, and satisfaction.

Our wide variety of products comprises different types of Storage Tanks such as Water Storage Tanks, Jacketed Water Tanks, Insulated Water Tank, Edible Oil Storage Tanks, Petroleum Storage Tanks, Reactors and Agitators, Heat Exchangers, Distillation Columns, Pressure Vessels, Acoustic Enclosures, Noise Test Chambers, Customised Acoustic Container for In-Plant Offices, Acoustic Booths, Homes, Offices, Rest Rooms, Lunch Rooms, and Conference Rooms.

We Manufacture Pressure Parts, Gas Tanks, Steel Columns, Bag Filters, Screw Conveyors Melting, Tower and Holding Furnaces, Loader Assembly, Structural Fabrication, Travelling Grate Frame Assembly, Pipe Support, Skid Assembly, Oil Separator, Shell Assembly, Dish End Assembly and Ducting Parts, and various parts of Indian Railway in Stainless Steel and Mild Steel.

OUR CORE VALUES

Anaveey Engineering Pvt. Ltd. is a values-driven organization. We embody our values when working with customers, suppliers, and employees. The five core values underpinning the way we do business are:

Integrity

The value of integrity is core to Anaveey. We act with integrity. We work together with trust, transparency, and honesty in everything we do.

Customer

We promote customer-driven corporate culture. We value and delight our customers by providing the best quality products. We put our customers first.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do. We are passionate for achieving the highest standards of quality. We care deeply about our work, about providing excellent customer service, and we truly care about achieving results for our clients.

Employee Development

We invest in the development of employees, enable continuous learning, and build caring and collaborative relationships based on trust and mutual respect.

Teamwork

We work in a teamwork environment considering one company, one team. Every employee of our company is a valued member, encouraged to contribute, appreciated for his/her efforts, and treated with respect. We know that the best work is not produced by individuals, but by enjoying collaboration as a team and supporting each other. We extend this team mentality to our customers and suppliers as we approach every business relationship as a partnership and work collaboratively with each other to meet our goals.
