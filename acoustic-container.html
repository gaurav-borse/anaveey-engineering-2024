Acoustic Container

Our Soundproof containers are ideal solution for industrial, commercial and residential spaces to preserve surrounding environment from noise pollution. These containers play a crucial role in minimizing sound transmission and creating quieter environments. We provide customized soundproof containers for In-plant Offices, Control Room, Operator Booth, Control Pulpit, Wet Labs, Music Practice Room, Professional Studios etc.

In-plant offices

In-plant offices are acoustic enclosures designed to provide administrative workspace for workers and supervisors inside the plant or factory, near the manufacturing operations. Modular in-plant offices can be prefabricated and quickly assembled on-site. They can even be expanded.

This type of acoustic enclosure will provide the sound isolation you need while also offering a close look at operations that will positively impact productivity. We meet a wide range of STC levels, as high as STC 55, depending on your needs.

This type of acoustic enclosure is also suitable for in-plant lunchrooms, break rooms or conference rooms. They can be customized in multiple sizes for your in-plant offices, from the small supervisor office to an entire soundproof workplace.

Acoustic Booths

Sound Proof booths are prepared according to customized requirements. Booth are designed for different purposes like recording booth, audiology booth, machine testing booth, noise test chamber etc.
