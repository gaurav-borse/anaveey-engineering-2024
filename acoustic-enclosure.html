Acoustic Enclosure

Our expertise and knowledge allow us to provide custom design and manufacturing of acoustic enclosures. Acoustic enclosures play a vital role in noise reduction, protecting both the equipment and the surrounding environment. These enclosures are crucial for maintaining quieter environments in various settings.

These sound enclosures are designed to suppress the loud noises from generators, compressors, and transformers.

Key Features
  • Body: Structure able to sustain upto 20Ton load. Material used for Outer sheet - Mild Steel, Stainless Steel, or Ferritic Steel
  • Inner side walls: Acoustic Insulation
  • Foam/ Rockwool Specs: Density: 28 to 400 kg/m3
  • Fire Resistance: Fire-resistant upto 120 min flammability
Acoustic Enclosures for Machinery and Equipment

Our acoustic expertise and manufacturing capabilities enable us to design and develop customized acoustic enclosures for industrial machinery and equipment with vibration-damping systems, appropriate visibility, acoustically treated ventilation systems, access hatches and openings, sliding oversized doors, or any other requirement. We provide noise reduction requirements from STC 25 to 55 decibels. We can even reach STC-70.

Acoustic Enclosures for Transformers

Our acoustic enclosures for transformers are tailor-made solutions to reduce humming noise from transformers, synchronous compensators, deicers, and other noisy equipment. Built with laboratory-tested absorbent steel panels, they are highly efficient at low frequencies.

We offer enclosures for all kind of noise sources such as :

  • Gas turbines Steam turbines
  • Compressors
  • Diesel Generator
  • Pumps
  • Conveyor
  • Fin Press Machine
  • Air Compressor
  • Forging Machine
  • CNC Machine
  • Scarfing Machine
  • Other Noise Generating Industrial Machines
