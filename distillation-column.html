Distillation Column

Anaveey is a leading expert and solutions provider for a wide range of distillation technologies. We are expert in design & manufacturing of distillation columns as per customer’s requirements. We offer Columns for Batch, Continuous, Stripping, Azeotropic, Extractive Distillation Process along with unique and extensive range of top-performance, hi-tech column internals.

Our distillation columns are widely used in the food, pharmaceutical and chemical industries. As a result of this process, with the distillation column it is possible to separate the dissolved solid phases or the gases present in a liquid mixture, by means of its vaporization and condensation.

We manufacture and supply distillation columns upto 5000L capacity.

Characteristics Of Distillation Column
