Heat Exchanger

Anaveey Engineering is a leading manufacturer of Shell and Tube-type Heat Exchangers that are primarily used for Condenser and Evaporator applications.

We provide tailored solutions to our clients by understanding their requirements. We manufacture high-quality, cost-effective Heat Exchangers in a wide variety of sizes & materials based on strategic planning, best practices, customer feedback, and reliable business processes.

We have complete facility to design and manufacture a wide range of Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers for cooling, condensing, and heating applications.

Technical Specifications
  • Type : Fixed Tube Sheet, Removable Tube Bundle, U-Tube, Coil in Tank, Double Pipe
  • Capacity Range : As per client requirement
  • Shell Diameter : 4″ to 125″
  • Tube Length : 1 M to 30 M
  • Tubes : Plain, Corrugated, Integrally Finned or U-Tubes
  • M.O.C. Tubes : Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Copper, Adm. Brass,
  • Aluminium, Titanium, Tantalum, Hastolloy, Alluminium Bronze, monel, Duplex, Cupronickle
  • Shell : Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Copper, Monel. Brass, Aluminum
