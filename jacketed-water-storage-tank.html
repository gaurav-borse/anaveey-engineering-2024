Jacketed Water Storage Tank

Anaveey Engineering is a leading manufacturer of Jacketed Tanks. The Jacketed Tanks are a high-quality range of storage tanks. These tanks are made from high- grade material which ensures high durability. These tanks are used for mixing and storing two or more fluids together. These tanks have a double wall design, with an outer layer that allows for temperature control through the circulation of a heating or cooling fluid. They are used in various industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemical manufacturing.

We offer the highest quality and affordable cost Jacketed Tanks  to our customers. We manufacture Jacketed Tanks  in various sizes and specifications to fulfil the clients' requirements.

We manufacture following types of Jacketed Tanks:
1. Conventional Jacket Tank

The main tank is outfitted with an outer wall that encapsulates it like a secondary vessel, and hot or cold media is circulated between the main tank and the jacket. It is typically the heaviest and thickest jacketed tank option.

2. Dimpled Jacket Tank

A dimple jacket tank features a lightweight outer shell that is welded to the main tank every 2” to 3” so the exterior looks like the dimples on a golf ball. The dimples form a network of pockets through which heating or cooling media can be circulated. A dimple jacket tank can often be the most economical way to add external temperature control to a storage tank or pressure vessel.

3. Half-pipe Jacket Tank

For this type of jacket, a length of half pipe is wrapped around the tank in a helical arrangement. Heating or cooling media is then circulated through the pipe raising or lowering the temperature of the tank.

Some of the benefits of acquiring a half-pipe jacketed tank include:

  • These tanks can hold more volume of a heating and cooling medium than a dimple-jacketed tank.
  • They are a less expensive option than conventional jackets.
  • Jackets can be applied to tanks built out of stainless steel, carbon steel, or higher alloys.
  • We can also equip your tank with internal coils to provide additional heating or cooling capability.
