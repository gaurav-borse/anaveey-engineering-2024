Pressure Vessel

Pressure vessels are enclosed containers that hold liquids, vapours, and gases at a pressure significantly higher or lower than the ambient pressure. These vessels play a crucial role in various industries due to their robust construction and ability to withstand high-pressure circumstances. Pressure vessels are used in Pharmaceutical, Cosmetic, Chemical, Food Processing and Dairy Processing Facilities.

At Anaveey Engineering, we are pleased to provide the highest-quality pressure vessels. Our expertise and knowledge allow us to provide custom design and fabrication of pressure vessels. Our specialists are capable of producing superior products to the most exacting specifications.

Pressure vessel specification
  • Available Size: 50-20,000 Liters
  • MOCs: 304 / 316 / 316l, Inc 625, Inc 800HT, Hastelloy C 276 etc.
  • Thickness: 3mm - 50 mm
  • Accessories: Pressure Gauge, Safety Valve, Drain valve and all other accessories.
