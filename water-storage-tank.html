Storage Tank

Water storage tanks are used to store water and provide faster access to a water supply. Anaveey Engineering is a leading manufacturer of Water Storage Tanks. We offer the highest quality and affordable cost chemical plant equipment to our customers.

Water storage tanks come in a range of materials, shapes, and sizes, depending on the intended use of the tank. We manufacture Water Storage Tanks upto 1,00,000 Liters capacity.

Water Storage Tanks are used in following sectors:
  • Agriculture
  • Commercial food and beverage preparation
  • Irrigation
  • Industrial manufacturing
  • Fire suppression

The tank’s size, materials, and specifications will be tailored to its intended use and water demands.

We offer following Water Storage Tanks:
  • Horizontal Water Storage Tank
  • Vertical Water Storage Tank
  • Conical Bottom Water Storage Tank
  • Conical Top Water Storage tank
  • Industrial Water Storage Tanks
  • Customized Water Storage Tanks
  • Cylindrical Water Tank
